Overview

Purpose

The purpose of a personal portfolio website is to showcase your skills, experience, and projects in a professional and visually appealing way. It serves as an online resume and personal brand to help you stand out to potential employers, clients, or collaborators.

Audience

Employers will visit my site to learn more about my background, skills, and experience in computer science and IT. The website will serve as a digital resume and portfolio, showcasing my technical projects, work history, and professional journey. Visitors will expect to find information about my technical skills, previous work experience, completed projects, and contact details. They might also look for my education background, certifications, and the technologies I specialize in. The website should be easy to navigate, visually appealing, and provide a clear overview of my skills and experience.
